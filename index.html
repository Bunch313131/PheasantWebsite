Competition Format

Main Competition

Two-man teams compete in five 9-hole matches in Four Ball Match Play Format (85% Handicap).

2 pts Hole Won
1 pt Hole Halved
2 pts Match Win Bonus
20 pts Total Per Match

All 9 holes are played in each match. After all five matches, the team with the most points in their flight advances as Flight Winner. The 2nd place team advances as Flight Runner-Up. Both advance to the Championship Horse Race.

Championship Horse Race

Alternate shot from the White tees, 50% team handicap (rounded down), strokes as they fall on the card. Continuous putting from closest to furthest. Teammates alternate tee shots hole to hole.

Hole 6
12 Flight Winners play — 4 highest scores eliminated
Hole 17
12 Flight Runners-Up play — 8 highest scores eliminated
Hole 7
12 remaining teams combine — 6 highest scores eliminated
Hole 8
6 teams remain — 3 highest scores eliminated
Hole 9
Final 3 teams play for Win, Place & Show

The winning team is crowned 2026 Pheasant Invitational Champions. Chip-offs break all ties.

Optional Competitions

Par 3 Contest

Thursday, 8:30 AM · $50/team

9-hole par 3 course competition to kick off the tournament.

Open Horse Race

Thursday, 3:15 PM · $50/team

Alternate shot elimination format. Field split into 4 groups across different holes. Top 2 from each group advance to finals on Holes 6–9. Elimination each hole down to a final 3 for Win, Place & Show.

Putting Contest

Thursday – Saturday · First attempt free, $5 additional

9-hole qualifying (most aces). Top 4 advance to Saturday semifinals. Top 2 from semis play the championship round. Enter as many times as you like. Top 4 finishers are in the money.

Team Long Drive

Thursday – Friday · First attempt free, $10/team additional

Measured by TrackMan / GC Quad. Combined fairway distance plus age of both players determines total. Enter multiple times. Top 5 finishers are in the money.

Rules & Handicaps

Governing Rules

Competition decisions follow NCGA rules and guidance.

Handicap Requirements

All competitors must have established handicaps using GHIN or equivalent. Handicaps are based on each competitor's low handicap for the prior 6 months at 90% per NCGA guidance.

Match Play Handicap

Main competition matches use 85% handicap. The Championship Horse Race uses 50% of team handicap (rounded down).

Maximum Differential

Maximum handicap differential for a team is 10. Teams may have a greater differential, but the high handicap player will be capped.

Committee Authority

The Pheasant Committee reserves the right to adjust any handicap it deems fitting to ensure competitive integrity, both before and during the tournament.

Substitutions

Substitutions are not allowed once the competition has begun.

Withdrawal

If a team is forced to withdraw during the event, all of their prior and future matches will be scored 0–20.

Tiebreakers

Flight winner tiebreaker: head to head, scorecard playoff, chip-off. Horse race ties broken by chip-off.
